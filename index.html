I'm an Adjunct Professor at the Federal University of Jataí (UFJ) and a Ph.D. student from the Centre of Informatics (CIn) of the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE). He was graduated in Information Systems from the Catholic University of Pernambuco (UNICAP). His master's subject was in Knowledge Representation at the University of São Paulo (USP). He is vice coordinator of Interest Group on Computing Education (GIEC) of the Brazilian Computer Society (SBC). He is a member of the Special Interest Group on Computer Science Education (SIGCSE) of ACM (Association for Computing Machinery). He is a member of the Research Group of Educational Development of Sustainable Multimedias (DEMULTS) of Federal Rural University of Pernambuco (UFRPE). He is member-facilitator of Diversity Comittee (CoDi/CIn). He is the member-facilitator of Working Group of Inclusion, Diversity, Equity, and Accessibility (WG IDEA) of GIEC/SBC. He is a member of the Research Group of Informatics on Education (NINE-IFG). He was chair of the Jataí ACM SIGCSE Chapter (2018-2023). He was a member of the Management Committee of CEIE (Special Commission of Informatics on Education) of SBC (2017-2020).
